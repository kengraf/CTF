# Starting a student club
This a a collection of ideas for club activities I had used over the years. For high school and college level.  

The biggest challenge is keeping everyone interested when you have both "noobs" and "nerds"  

Some more advanced students might gravitate to a teacher or mentor role but most will want to be technically challenged.  I have found having "seniors" run an event for "freshmen" works well.  

Online: individual student
https://www.uscyberchallenge.org/  runs online quests and camps
https://www.cybermission.tech/  slightly more advanced, can work as a team event
https://nationalcyberleague.org/  annual competition.
https://kc7cyber.com/
